> Thirty-six billion of the dead wake naked and twenty-five years old on the banks of a
> River ten million miles long. Grailstones feed them; death only moves them elsewhere
> up the River. **Someone built this.** The quest is to reach the tower at the headwaters
> and learn who — and why.

It is, in a sense, the [Library](https://github.com/DavidWise01/library) made flesh:
Burton and Twain and Gilgamesh and Alice, every named figure of history, awake again on
one bank — and what they *do* with the second life is the whole point.

## The roll

### 🜄 The Seekers — questing up-River for the truth
- **Sir Richard Francis Burton** — explorer and swordsman; the first to wake too early; will not stop until the tower.
- **Alice Hargreaves** — the real Alice of Wonderland, twenty-five again; Burton's first companion.
- **Peter Jairus Frigate** — the writer (and the author's shadow); chronicler of the quest.
- **Nur ed-Din el-Musafir** — Moorish Sufi master; the discipline the Ethicals came to fear.

### ⚙ The Builders — the riverboat and the long dream
- **Samuel Clemens (Mark Twain)** — bargains for meteorite-iron to build the *Not For Hire* and steam to the headwaters.
- **Lothar von Richthofen** — the Red Baron's brother; the dream's wings.
- **Milton Firebrass** — engineer of the impossible machine.

### ⚔ The Tyrants — who seize the River by force
- **King John** — steals Clemens's first riverboat and races him north; villainy resurrected unchanged.
- **The River Kingdoms** — the slaver-states; proof a second life is not a second soul.

### ✶ The Second Chance — redemption, and the refusal of violence
- **La Viro (Jacques Gillot)** — founds the Church of the Second Chance; violence forfeits the gift.
- **Hermann Göring** — tyrant and addict who claws toward redemption through the Church; the saga's hardest mercy.

### 🤝 The Companions — the loyal band from the first morning
- **Kazz** — a Paleolithic man, nearly the last of his kind; Burton's fiercest friend.
- **Monat Grrautut** — the Tau Cetan who once sterilized Earth, now wandering in sorrow; Burton's wisest friend.
- **Cyrano de Bergerac** — the finest blade and a finer poet than his legend.
- **Tom Mix** — the cowboy star, riding the bank like a back lot.
- **Gilgamesh** — king of Uruk, oldest of the named; twenty-five again on the grass.

### △ The Ethicals — the world-builders, the gods of Riverworld
- **The Council of Twelve** — the posthumans who raised everyone and watch from the tower.
- **The Agents** — operatives disguised among the resurrected, guarding the secret.

### ✦ The Renegade — *set apart, the hidden hand*
- **X · the Mysterious Stranger (Loga)** — one of the twelve who broke faith; woke Burton early, recruited the chosen few, and gave humanity a path to the truth. The renegade god on whom the whole saga secretly turns.
